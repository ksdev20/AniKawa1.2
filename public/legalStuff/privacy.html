Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 20/6/2025

At AniFlow, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how it is protected. By using the website, you agree to this policy.

1. Data Collection

We collect data only from users who register and log in. We do not collect any personal information from non-logged-in visitors.

For registered users, we collect the following data:

  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted using bcrypt)
  • Profile picture and banner (defaults are used unless customized)
  • Watchlist (user-selected anime)
  • Viewing history (anime you've watched)

2. How Your Data is Stored

Your data is stored securely in our MongoDB database. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and are never stored in plain text. Only your email and non-sensitive profile data are accessible via secure queries.

3. Cookies

We set a single HTTP-only cookie called userID after you log in. This cookie is used only to keep you logged in and expires automatically after 7 days. No tracking cookies or third-party cookies are used.

4. Local Storage

We use local storage on your browser to cache anime-related data or personal UI settings. No private data is stored here.

5. Embedded Content

Some videos on this site are embedded from AniOneAsia's official YouTube channel. When you play these videos, YouTube may collect your viewing behavior in accordance with its own Privacy Policy. We do not control or track this data.

6. Analytics (Optional/Future)

In the future, we may integrate basic analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand how the site is used. If used, it will collect only anonymized data such as device type and page views. You will be able to opt out.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties. Your data remains private and is only used to support your experience on the platform.

8. Children's Privacy

This website is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you are a parent and believe your child has created an account, please contact us immediately.

9. Your Rights

You may request to delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us via the email below.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify users of major changes. Continued use of the site after updates implies acceptance.

11. Contact

If you have questions or concerns, please contact us at: supportaniflow12@gmail.com

This is a non-commercial, fan-created project using public data sources. We respect user privacy and comply with data protection best practices.
